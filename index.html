Watch Before You Start

The Loom video below walks through the France workflow end-to-end. Watch it first, then use the step-by-step instructions below as your reference while you set up each destination.

01 Library

Find your workflow

Click Workflows → Shared with Me. Open the folder for your package. Click the destination, then Make a Copy to add it to your personal library.

02 Trip View

Apply to a trip

Open the trip → Automations → Add Workflow. Select the destination. Automation dates populate from the trip start date — check them before activating anything.

Short-lead tripsIf a trip departs soon, switch triggers with a 150-day timing to Automation Added and set a day offset.
03 Timing

Adjust triggers as needed

Click any automation to edit. Switch from Trip Start to Automation Added → After Event with a day count if the original date has passed. System autosaves.

France example150-day trigger fires in March for an Aug trip — switch to After Event → 45 days to land Aug 2.
04 Review

Confirm your send schedule

Click Sort by Status. Active automations show confirmed send dates at the top. Inactive ones (EES, unused sub-destinations) remain visible but won't send until toggled on.

05 Email Templates

Customize a template (optional)

In the library go to Emails, open the destination folder, and click Make a Copy on any template you want to personalize — e.g. to add Amazon affiliate links. Rename it. Then in the workflow click the three dots on the automation → Delete Action → Send Email from Library and select your version. Set recipients to All Travelers — each individually, then toggle Active.

Section 01

How It All Connects

✉️

Email Templates

Pre-written, destination-specific emails are already in your Tern Library — covering inspiration, must-see/do highlights, transportation, money tips, and packing, timed for each stage of the planning journey.

⚡

Automations

Each workflow comes pre-populated with automations linked to the right email templates. Automations must be toggled to Active to send, and trigger timings are configurable per trip.

🗂️

Workflows

Destination workflows bundle all automations and tasks together. They have been built for you — apply a workflow to a trip and the full email sequence is ready to configure and activate.

🏷️

Auto-Apply Tags

Once you're comfortable, enable auto-apply: tag a trip france or uk and the matching workflow applies automatically.

Recommendation — start manually We recommend manually adding workflows to trips for the first several bookings, until you're fully comfortable reviewing and updating the automation trigger timings. Once the process feels routine, enable the auto-apply tag trigger to run hands-free going forward.

All automations are set by default to fire relative to the trip start date. The sequence below reflects the intended arc — from early inspiration through entry requirements, logistics, and a final send-off. Each timing can be adjusted per trip as needed.

Adjusting for short-lead trips If a booking comes in with less than 150 days to departure, some triggers will already be in the past and won't fire. Switch those automations from Trip Start to Automation Added → After Event and set a day offset that puts the send date within the remaining timeline. Section 05

Amazon Affiliate Links

Your email templates are built with passive income in mind. Three types of emails contain affiliate link placeholders — all links have been stripped from your templates and need to be replaced with your own before activating.

10%

Commission potential

Amazon Associates is free to join and offers up to 10% commission on qualifying purchases. Luggage and travel accessories earn 7%; guidebooks 4.5%. Your packing and inspiration emails are a natural fit — clients already trust your recommendations.

Inspiration Emails — Storefront Link

Each inspiration email links clients to "your Amazon page." Replace this with your personal Amazon storefront URL — a curated page of your favorite travel products.

Must See/Do Emails — Guidebook Links

Link to recommended guidebooks: Lonely Planet, Rick Steves, DK Eyewitness, Moon Travel Guides. Each title gets its own unique affiliate link.

Packing Emails — Product Links

UK & Europe packing list emails include slots for packing cubes, travel adapters (Type G), rain layers, crossbody bags, money belts, and portable chargers.

Update Once, Apply Everywhere

Edit a template in your Library and Tern propagates the change to all automations using it — update your affiliate links once and every future client gets the refreshed version.

How to create your Amazon Associates account

1

Sign up at Amazon Associates

Go to affiliate-program.amazon.com/signup. The program is free — no upfront cost. Use your existing Amazon account or create one.

2

Enter your website or platform URL

Your business website, Facebook page, or Instagram handle all qualify. Enter whichever platform you use to communicate with clients.

3

Create your Store ID (affiliate tag)

Choose something recognizable — like your business name. This ID is embedded in every link you share. Example: bookedbeyond-20

4

Set up your Amazon Storefront

Once approved, create a storefront in Associates Central organized by category (e.g. "UK Packing Favorites", "France Travel Essentials"). This is the page you link to in inspiration emails.

5

Make 3 qualifying sales within 180 days

Amazon requires at least 3 sales in your first 180 days. Your packing list and Must See/Do emails are your fastest path — they go to every booked client automatically.

6

Generate links and update your templates

Use the SiteStripe toolbar (appears at the top of Amazon.com when logged into Associates) to get a short affiliate link for any product. Then update each template in Tern's Library.

Section 06

Setup Tips & Troubleshooting

🔘 Activating automations

Every automation has an Active/Inactive toggle. Automations must be Active to send automatically. If you're still building or testing, leave them Inactive. Use Task 2 ("Adjust & activate automation timings") as your reminder to go back and turn them on.

⚠️ Bulk apply warning

Bulk Apply fires immediately. If "Set automation to active when applied" is on, every existing trip in the batch will trigger the automation right away. Turn that setting off before running a Bulk Apply if you're not ready for emails to send.

📅 Trigger dates already passed?

If you apply a workflow and the trigger date (e.g. "150 days before trip") has already passed, that automation will not fire. Switch the trigger to Automation Added → After Event with a day offset to bring it back into the trip window.

🏷️ Tags drive everything

Tag a trip uk → UK workflow applies. Tag a trip france → France workflow applies. Tags can be added when creating the trip or at any point after — the workflow fires the moment the tag is added. You can apply multiple workflows to one trip.

✏️ Updating email templates

When you edit a template in your Library, Tern can propagate that change to all automations and workflows already using it. Update affiliate links, refresh content, or add new tips — the change ripples out everywhere automatically.

📧 Email recipients

For automations triggered by trip dates, choose recipients: All travelers with Full Access or All travelers. Make sure clients are added as travelers with Full Access before the automation fires, or they won't receive the email.

Quick checklist
  • Always Make a Copy before editing any shared template or workflow
  • Rename your copies clearly — include a custom note or your name
  • Check populated automation dates after applying any workflow
  • Sort by Status to confirm what's active and what's scheduled
  • Use the three-dot menu to swap emails in a workflow without rebuilding it
  • Leave EU Entry Requirements (EES) inactive until the official EU launch date is confirmed
  • Add affiliate link disclosure language to your email footers
